abstract: "This paper addresses the multiobjective traveling salesman–repairman problem with profits (MTSRPP), a rich vehicle routing variant that combines multiple conflicting objectives with the collection of optional profits. We design and implement a variable neighborhood descent (VND) algorithm tailored to solve real scenarios arising in the logistics sector. Computational experiments on real instances from major Spanish companies demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed approach."
